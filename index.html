    About me

    I am PhD student working in the field of exoplanets, mainly focusing on transmission spectroscopy in visible wavelengths. For that, I am using high-resolution spectrographs like HARPS and ESPRESSO. My main focus is on the sodium doublet lines, which has been detected in atmospheres of many hot Jupiters.

    I grew up in Czech republic (Prague), where I did my Bachelor's study in general physics, before moving in Geneva for my Master and PhD to study exoplanets.

    In my free-time, I love playing games, both PC and board ones (especially strategies and RPGs). Otherwise I am also interested in anime/manga and am learning Japanese.
